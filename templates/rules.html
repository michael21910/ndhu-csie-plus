Q: What is NDHU CSIEplus?
NDHU CSIEplus is a question-and-answer website. You can watch the questions and answers from everyone on this platform.
For those who like to answer questions from others, there are always endless questions on the website; for those who have questions to ask, there are always enthusiastic students on NDHU CSIEplus to help them out. Moreover, if there is an in-depth question, there will often be many intense and exciting discussions about the question, which is a lot for the questioner.
What's even better, since NDHU CSIEplus is very close to students' university life, for some answers that only students at NDHU know, you cannot find a feature like this on any other websites!
Become a member to ask and answer questions!
Q: What is the difference between before and after logging in?
If you log in, you can ask and answer questions on NDHU CSIEplus! However, whether you log in, you can watch all the questions and answers on NDHU CSIEplus!
Q: What does the token do send to me by email after registration?
You will use the token if you want to change your user password. Therefore, please keep it safe and don't let anyone know!
Q: What type of question is available in NDHU CSIEplus?
Any questions about Computer Science and course related to it are all available!
Q: What do points do?
You will get 100 points after registration. If you answer a question, you will gain 15 points. If you ask a question, your points will be deducted by 10. See you on the leaderboard!
Q: Do I have to pay for this?
No, you don't have to pay for this question-and-answer website! Start your journey and be our member right now!
