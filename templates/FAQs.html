對於那些喜歡回答以及幫助別人的人，網路上的問題是永無止盡的；對於那些需要問問題的同學們，總是會有充滿熱情的同學們解決他們的問題。此外，如果有一個問得非常深入的問題，大家將會有一個非常精彩的討論，這無疑對於發問的同學是很有幫助的。

成為會員並開始提問以及回答問題吧！
Q1: What is NDHU CSIEplus?
NDHU CSIEplus is a Q&A website. You can watch the questions and answers from everyone on this platform.
For those who like to answer questions from others, there are always endless questions on the website; for those who have questions to ask, there are always enthusiastic students on NDHU CSIEplus to help them out. Moreover, if there is an in-depth question, there will often be many intense and exciting discussions about the question, which is a lot for the questioner.
What's even better, since NDHU CSIEplus is very close to students' university life, for some answers that only students at NDHU know, you cannot find a feature like this on any other websites!
Become a member to ask and answer questions!
Q2: 在NDHU CSIEplus可以提問哪些種類的問題?
所有關於資訊工程的問題都可以提問！
為了確保平台上的問題都是與資訊工程相關，我們訓練了一個基於Word2Vec以及CNN技術的模型。
這個模型會在你提問問題時判斷你發的文章是不是跟資訊工程相關，如果不相關，你就無法提問成功！
Q2: What type of question is available in NDHU CSIEplus?
Any questions about Computer Science and course related 0to it are all available!
To ensure that the questions are all related to Computer Science, we trained a model based on Word2Vec and CNN.
This model will detect whether the question you ask is related to Computer Science or not, and if not, you will not be able to ask the question successfully!
Q3: 登入前後有什麼差別嗎?
如果你登入了，你就可以提問以及回答問題。
不過，不論你有沒有登入，你都可以在這個平台上看到所有的問題以及回答。
Q3: What is the difference between before and after logging in?
If you log in, you can ask and answer questions on NDHU CSIEplus! However, whether you log in, you can watch all the questions and answers on NDHU CSIEplus!
Q4: 我的帳號以及密碼是可以被保障的嗎?
可以的，對於所有使用者的密碼，我們一律採用加密的方式，以防止任何人可以直接看到你的密碼。
Q4: Can my account and password be safe?
Yes, for all users' passwords, we use encryption to ensure that nobody can see your password directly.
Q5: 註冊之後發給我的信件中的token是甚麼東西?
在更改密碼時會使用到發給你的token，請妥善保管並且不要洩漏出去！
Q5: What does the token do send to me by email after registration?
You will use the token if you want to change your user password. Therefore, please keep it safe and don't let anyone know!
Q6: 點數系統是甚麼?
在註冊之後你會獲得100點。
如果你回答了一個問題，你將會獲得15點；如果你提問了一個問題，你將會被扣除10點。
期待在排名列表上看到你的名字！
Q6: What do points do?
You will get 100 points after registration. If you answer a question, you will gain 15 points. If you ask a question, your points will be deducted by 10. See you on the leaderboard!

你不需要在這個平台上付錢。這個平台是完全免費的！
Q7: Do I have to pay for this?
No, you don't have to pay for this. This is completely free!
